ABOUT US

We made world's best chicken and blue meth

Los Pollos Hermanos is a regional chain of fried chicken restaurants founded and owned by Gus Fring. The chain is highly regarded for its delicious food, exceptional customer service, and spotless reputation. The bright and welcoming branding makes it the perfect cover for his illegal operations. Gus uses the restaurants as a front to launder drug money. The legitimate cash flow from the thriving business helps mask the illicit income. Additionally, the restaurants provide a logistical advantage, as they serve as hubs for distributing methamphetamine hidden in the chicken batter or other shipments.Gus is seen as a philanthropic businessman, often donating to the community and supporting law enforcement causes, such as funding the DEA. This cements his image as a respected figure in Albuquerque, deflecting suspicion from his criminal activities.
